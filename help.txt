-mute <user>
	mutes the mentioned user

-unmute <user>
	Unmutes the mentioned user

-vcmute
	mutes everyone in the vc you are in

-vcunmute
	unmutes everyone in the vc you are in

-vcmove <Users>
	Moves specified users to specified vc

-vcmoveall <current channel name or id> <Id or name of channel to be moved to>
	moves everyone from first channel to second
